ABOUT

Ultimate Frisbee is an exciting, fast-paced team sport played with a flying disc, or Frisbee. It’s a non-contact game that combines the best elements of soccer, basketball, and football but with a unique twist – it’s played with a frisbee! Each team requires seven players on the field whose goal is to score points by bringing the frisbee from one end of the field to the other. It may sound easy, but the sport calls for strategic passing, consistent cutting, and most importantly, solid teamwork!

GAMEPLAY

The Pull

Each team lines up in their respective end zones. The game begins when one team throws (or "pulls") the Frisbee to the other team to start play.

Passing

The team with possession tries to advance the frisbee down the field by passing it to teammates. Players cannot run with the disc; they must establish a pivot foot after catching it.

Catching

Teammates must catch passes to keep possession. If a pass is dropped, intercepted, or goes out of bounds, it results in a turnover and the opposing team gains possession.

SCORING AT THE ENDZONE

The objective is to catch the frisbee in the opposing team’s end zone. Each catch in the end zone scores one point.
